The party arrives at [[Sage's Hollow]] (S1-2), a small, strange village with a theocratic community and a contaminated water supply blamed on the nearby Blue Cap village of [[Cofgod]]. They settle in, meet the fey creature [[Bloody-Feet Morsch]], who pressures them to investigate the Sage's Cairn, and head into the forest.

**Into the Caves of Cofgod** (S3-4), the party clears the Blue Caps of wrongdoing, fights through a faerie dragon's lair, and discovers a bull-headed being, an Aasimar named [[Alorus]], chained in a laboratory with infernal instruments. They free him. A devil named Baato immediately contacts them through Alorus, marking the party as targets. The village of Sage's Hollow refuses to shelter Alorus, and the north road is already blockaded by fiends.

**Fleeing South** (S5), the party is guided to the border town of [[Gristwell]], where Matron Hestia Grinwald shelters them for a night. She performs [[Cineromancy]] and reads ash fortunes for each party member and points them south through the forest path to avoid the fiend's blockade.

**The Coast** (S6-7), they emerge near Southpoint Lighthouse, which has been seized by Hadozee pirates called the [[Mad Monkeys]], led by Captain Escanor de Simiano. The party clears the lighthouse and discovers the pirates were smuggling humanoid automaton parts into [[Catameria]] under orders from someone signed "R.R.R." On the ship, they confirm the crates bear the crest of House [[Meredane]]. A wandering elven seer named Taleris warns them against alerting the city guard.

**Through the Forest** (S8-9), the party crosses a corrupted clearing full of bodies, attacked by a Harrower and a construct that mirrors [[Cogsworth]]. [[Bloody-Feet Morsch]] is revealed to have been hiding in [[Adelaide]]'s pack the whole time and cheerfully departs. The party reaches [[Maere]], a junction town, where they gather rumors about the Winter Trials and a sick queen in [[Catameria]] before passing through the gates.

**Arriving in Catameria** (S10-11), they settle into [[Brennly Wriggle]]'s house in the [[Lantern Ward]]. [[Atar]] investigates [[Alorus]]'s nature and confirms his magic is innate, not bestowed, and that it serves as the source of [[Adelaide]]'s own sorcery. She had believed her magic to be a gift from the Sage. While buying food from Donald O'Micks, [[Cogsworth]] spots a missing persons poster for Sgt. Artos Lemm, the friendly border guard from [[Gatebridge]]. The party resolves to find him.

**The Sewer Snatcher** (S12), they track Artos through the sewers with the help of [[Dimming]] journalist Frankie Pierce, navigating [[Gravel Rats]] territory before finding a jail and a drug operation. They end the session bargaining with Chaos, an imprisoned tiefling slaver with knowledge of Artos's location.

**The Winter Trials** (S13-14), the party enters the city's adventuring tournament under the name the Thunder Cats. They meet [[Jaspar]] Delaware, a pixie cartographer, who joins them. A devil disguised as a tiefling named [[Kaelis]] confronts [[Lucky]], revealing a devil's mark on his hand. Back at Brennly's, [[Atar]] uncovers relics in the study: a devil's grimoire called the [[Pride Epitome]], the Grey King's petrified phalange, and soul coins wrapped in an infernal letter, suggesting Brennly was tied to a devil's plot. [[Rennin III Meredane]] drunkenly confronts the party in the street.

**Round Two** (S15), the Thunder Cats face a flaming elemental encounter designed by Iyra Valcaryn, a mage of House [[Valcaryn]]. Mid-fight, Iyra speaks through the creature and requests a private meeting. At the Grey Lance, a Greylinn-themed bar, she warns the party about House Meredane and directs them to Halandar's Antiques to find buried information.

**Wall Town** (S16), they descend into the underground black market of [[Wall Town]]. At the abandoned shop of Halandar and LaVey, they uncover a letter connecting a devil named Baeldric Vauldra to [[Oswyn LaVey]]. They then witness Fortuna Mortem, Capo of the [[Gravel Rats]], publicly announce bounties at the Manse de Mortem. The Thundercats are named with a bounty of 500 gold each, 1000 for Lucky.

**The Third Trial** (S17-18), the party faces a drider in magical darkness alongside light-eating fairies that coalesce into a hivemind. Mid-fight, three bombs disguised as a civilian explodes in the stands, a joint attack by House [Meredane] and the [[Gravel Rats]]. Afterward, the Grand Seneschal of [[Catameria]], [[Vurn Orleth]], requests a meeting. The party learns that the terrorist attack was a message from the Gravel Rats. Mid meeting, the meeting is interrupted by Baeldric Vauldra ([[Baato]]), a supposed friend of the Seneschal. The party leaves, escorted to [[Brennly's Home]] by some members of the royal guard. Once the party has had time to rest, an attack begins on the home. Edward Crowley of the Gravel Rats, 6 Elite Aarokocra assassins, 24 rallied rat runners... And one Renk. 

**Night of a Thousand-Man Swarm**: The party kills the next Gravel Rat attacking the house, but the last words of a runner alert the party to imminent danger. There are 24 hours until safety is guaranteed by the next stage of the Winter Trials. No where is safe, and the rats are swarming.
