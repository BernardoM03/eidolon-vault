## Vurn Orleth
[[Vurn Orleth]], the Grand Seneschal of Catameria has requested an audience with the party. In the aftermath of a terrorist attack on the Heroes Colosseum, he revealed to a message from the Gravel Rats. Three automaton heads that repeat their own individual words. **Rat. Kill. Cats**. The session ends at the table, discussing the now apparent tie between the Gravel Rats and House Meredane (Gravel Rat message deployed through Automaton, party confirmed that the automaton has a tie to House Meredane).

### Goals for this conversation
Vurn believed every word they said, his eye confirmed it, but believing someone and trusting them are different things. He needs to know what they are going to do with what this information, because if they move recklessly against Meredane without his control, they will make the problem worse. He wants them contained, not silenced.

1. "Now I will ask you plainly. With this information, what do you plan to do?"
	1. "I called you here because you are standing in the middle of something that we do not fully understand, and people in that position tend to get other people killed. It is my job to not let other people get killed. So I ask you again, what is your plan."
	2. "Surely you do not plan to keep yourselves safe or uncover these problems... without a plan... do you?" He looks, with the first time in this conversation, baffled.
