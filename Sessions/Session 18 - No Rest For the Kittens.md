## Vurn Orleth
[[Vurn Orleth]], the Grand Seneschal of Catameria has requested an audience with the party. In the aftermath of a terrorist attack on the Heroes Colosseum, he revealed to a message from the Gravel Rats. Three automaton heads that repeat their own individual words. **Rat. Kill. Cats**. The session ends at the table, discussing the now apparent tie between the Gravel Rats and House Meredane (Gravel Rat message deployed through Automaton, party confirmed that the automaton has a tie to House Meredane).

### Goals for this conversation
Vurn believed every word they said, his eye confirmed it, but believing someone and trusting them are different things. He needs to know what they are going to do with what this information, because if they move recklessly against Meredane without his control, they will make the problem worse. He wants them contained, not silenced.

1. "Now I will ask you plainly. With this information, what do you plan to do?"
	1. "I called you here because you are standing in the middle of something that we do not fully understand, and people in that position tend to get other people killed. It is my job to not let other people get killed. So I ask you again, what is your plan."
	2. "Surely you do not plan to keep yourselves safe or uncover these problems... without a plan... do you?" He looks, with the first time in this conversation, baffled.
2. "I can offer you four soldiers for escort to your residence. What you do after that is your own decision. I am not in the business of giving adventurers a leash."
3. "If you find something solid... something I can put in front of the Prince Consort without it being dismissed as hearsay, the Crown will be ready to act. You may present it to me next we meet at the Castella Grande."

### Baeldric Enters.
Mid sentence, Vurn stops and turns his eyes to the door. No announcement from the guards, but a gruff voice echoes through the room. “Where can an old tiefling get a drink and a seat around these parts. Does this room work?” A tiefling man steps in without haste. Older. Well dressed, but not in a courtly way. He smiles as if he is arriving late to a dinner.

Vurn does not stand. He does not reach for a weapon. He looks annoyed, but not surprised.

“Baeldric,” Vurn says. "Welcome to my important meeting regarding the terrorist attacks on the colosseum with the Thunder Cats."

Baeldric gives him a small nod, then lets out a small "hmmm" in process. His eyes drift to the party. The smile stays, but it sharpens like he expected as such. “Well. This is fun.” He moves closer.  "Let's play a game, why don't we." He announces. "I like to read to people, why "

Vurn will interject. "I apologize on his behalf, Thunder Cats. This is my friend, Baeldric Vauldra, a man with too much money and time on his hands, Please, you do not need humor his request."

"You're no fun" Then he points gently with his cane, first to Jasper.
