## The Grande Receptions

### Dressed to Impress.
Finalists are expected to wear the loaned Shift-weave formalwear (or equivalent finery) and display at least one mark of their team's identity: a sigil, a banner pin, a token from a sponsor. A character without appropriate dress or a visible team marker has Disadvantage on Charisma checks tonight; the other finalist teams and nobles read it as the team not taking the honor seriously.

### Renown
The reception unfolds in **three rounds**: Introductions/Reception, A cocktail hour, and Open Dancefloor, generally interspersed by those speaking about the Winter Trials.

During each round, characters must split into groups of 2 and can mingle, network, flatter, deflect prying questions, or pursue their own agenda. You will be mingling amongst public figures, nobles, and other finalists. Use your relevant skills to find information about other participants and create connections.
