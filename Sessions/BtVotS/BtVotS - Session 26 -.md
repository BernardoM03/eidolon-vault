## The First Challenge
As the party wakes up, they have a moment to themselves to prepare for whatever they think the first challenge will be.

After a moment, Terrence will visit the party's room to alert them of the first challenge. They must meet in the Library. He will answer no questions about what to expect, except that "The time to find info is over. Prepare for anything."

**Library of the Castella Grande**
*A vast library with a strong diverse array of topics ranging from fiction to regional ethnographies.*
- The room maintains the same architectural style as the rest of the house, with bookshelves structured into the clay and slate walls.
- Entering the room, you see about a dozen people. Vurn Orleth alongside a litany of scribes, one prominent looking mage, and the Laughing Gauntlet. 
- The party has a moment to interact with the room before the Grand Seneschal will lay a book in a center table, explaining the type of conjuration magic that will occur in a moments time. 
- The book will open to a page containing a large eye.

Lair Action - 20:
**The Eye Opens**
Once the eye is open, the creature closest to the center of the eye has it's magical effects increased. **Spells deal max hit dice and magical effects gain a DC of +5**. 

All other creatures feel intense power to be harnessed at the Center of the Eye.
### Gameplan
The Laughing Gauntlet knows about the Eye and has one priority: get Johan to center before Round 2. Carlos opens by hitting the most threatening PC and phasing out. Eliza uses Shatterspear to knock anyone off the center path, then Formation Calls Puyol into position beside Johan. Puyol puts Guardian on Johan immediately. Johan walks to center and Marks the party's healer/buffer. From there, Puyol holds position, Carlos cycles the backline, and Eliza handles anyone contesting center. They win by not moving.
### Johan the Laughing Gauntlet
112 HP, 17 AC, +7 Initiative
STR 12, DEX 14, CON 16, INT 13, WIS 12, CHA 22
**Setup & Punchline**: Target is marked until the end of their turn, garnering the audience of a thousand souls. At the end of their turn, they must make a DC 17 CHA save or take 6d8 psychic damage. Subtract damage done by the highest damage dealt to an individual target. If the creature takes the full 6d8, they are also Frightened of the Laughing Gauntlet Artifact.
**Mocking Chorus** (Bonus Action): Johan raises the Gauntlet and the souls within shriek with laughter. Each creature of his choice within 10ft must make a DC 17 CHA saving throw or take 2d6 psychic damage.
### Ironfront Puyol
157 HP, 21 AC, -1 Initiative
STR 20, DEX 8, CON 20, INT 12, WIS 14, CHA 8
**Ironfront**: One ally of choice within 30ft gains resistance to one single instance of damage they take before the start of Puyol's next turn.
**Guardian** (Bonus Action): Puyol plants themselves between their ally and the enemy. Until the start of Puyol's next turn, all attacks against one ally within 5ft of Puyol are made with disadvantage.
**Interpose**: (Reaction) When an ally within 15ft is targeted by an attack, Puyol moves up to 15ft toward that ally and becomes the new target of the attack. Puyol has resistance to all damage from that attack.
### Eliza 
97 HP, 17 AC, + 4 Initiative
STR 14, DEX 18, CON 14, 20 INT, 14 WIS, 10 CHA
**Tactical Position**: When a target is within 10ft of an Eliza and one of her allies, Eliza has advantage to attack them.
**Shatterspear**: +9 to Hit, Reach 10. 2d10+6 thunder damage. A creature struck by the spear must make a DC 17 STR saving throw or be pushed back 30ft.
**Formation Call (Bonus Action)**: One Laughing Gauntlet member within 60ft who can hear Eliza may immediately move up to half their speed without provoking opportunity attacks and make one attack.
**Strike Now (Legendary Action)**: Eliza commands one Laughing Gauntlet member within 60ft to make one attack or use one ability against any creature.
