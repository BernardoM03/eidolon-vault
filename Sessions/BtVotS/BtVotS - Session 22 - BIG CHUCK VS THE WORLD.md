## Fight with Big Chuck
Some would think that because Big Chuck has other's do his bidding, that he is incapable himself. That is the furthest possibility from the truth. He has his own obligations, to himself. To hone his genius and expertise, so that all may gaze upon him in awe and fear. To know that the best among them has been born, and to revel in his presence. But the problem with being the greatest, is that no one else is, even those he handpicked to uphold his legacy. 

Because of that, Big Chuck is furious. But, they are all just worms in the ground. There is no reason to be blinded by his rage, but there must be a way keep his reputation if he is to remain the Underground King of Catameria. A show of strength. A fair duel between Big Chuck and the perpetrators, to prove his superiority and get rid of those worms all at once. All he'll need is little bit of... motivation, perhaps a friend or loved one?


## A Counsel with The Grey King
Atar, in your wake and in your rest, you are wise enough to find yourself drifting to this newly appended artifact more than you should be. It doesn't seem to be impacting you, but you find it trying to draw you in. It has noticed your interest in it, and responds with an interest in you. In the night, feel a drawing pulse from within. It is strange, but it almost feels like an attunement procedure.

You feel your vision and consciousness fading from the material world, slowly and slowly until the burnt ruins of a house beside you are gone. You find yourself lying down on hard bright stones. Behind you, a large balcony extends outside, with two staircases going down on each side. Before you, a large staircase.
- This is the [[Divine Tower of Greylinn]], located in the [[Boz'Kumlar Desert]]. 
- If Atar begins to head down, he hears a echoing voice in his head. "Were you not here to speak with me?"

Upstairs, you find yourself in a wide and tall room. The walls must be hundreds of feet away, and the ceiling even taller. It is all made of the same bright marbled stone. Before you, you see a what resembles more of a dignified platform than a throne, wide and low to the floor. Atop, a dark swirling figure resembling a human with two distinct features. A three-pronged crown and a calcified finger, which seems like the only solid parts of it. This is the [[Grey King]].

[He speaks calmly and confidently, like that of an Elf in LotR]

Once Atar kneels, the Grey King will acknowledge him as a servant. One he acknowledges him as a servant, The Grey King will be more than happy to guide and help.
