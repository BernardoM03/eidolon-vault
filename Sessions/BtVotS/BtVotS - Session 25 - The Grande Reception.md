## The Grande Receptions

### Dressed to Impress.
Finalists are expected to wear the loaned Shift-weave formalwear (or equivalent finery) and display at least one mark of their team's identity: a sigil, a banner pin, a token from a sponsor. A character without appropriate dress or a visible team marker has Disadvantage on Charisma checks tonight.
- The guests of the reception will read it as the team not taking the honor seriously.
### Renown
The reception unfolds in **three rounds**: Introductions/Reception, A cocktail hour, and open dance floor, generally interspersed by those speaking about the Winter Trials.

During each round, characters must split into groups of 2 and can mingle, network, flatter, deflect prying questions, or pursue their own agenda. You will be mingling amongst public figures, nobles, and other finalists. Use your relevant skills to find information about other participants and create connections.
- Use insight to deem ways to appeal to guests
- Use your spell slots and charisma checks to properly appeal to those
- High rolls won't matter if you don't know what the person cares about
### The Ballroom
The ballroom door, large and sturdy wood, is wide open. Beyond you see a large polished floor of orange and cream colored ceramic will lit by braziers, and a set of two magnificent chandeliers.
- A large bar is connected to the south wall, with a door that seems to go into a kitchen
- An overlooking platform exists on the second floor, with railings that allow those to view from above.
- In the center the ballroom floor, currently occupied by guests, lots of notable people (see the following)

### Introductions
**Renoux** is on the ballroom floor, near the garden doors
- Wary/transactional; white-and-black pinstripe suit, ivory rings, polished tusks
- Standing: respects bluntness/results, not flattery (+1 for matching his tone; nothing for charm)

**Selene Vask**, the Captain of the Gold Rings, is in the Garden admiring a bushel of blue roses
- Ex-city watch, bought out of debt by Renoux; cold, disciplined, purely results-oriented
- Doesn't trash-talk, just watches and assesses
- Standing: +1 for direct/professional exchange; small talk gets polite non-answers, no effect

**Johann of the Laughing Gauntlet** is on the ballroom balcony with one other member of the Laughing Gauntlet, with a glass and a table of coin (evidently gambling)
- Loud, colorful armor, gambling-minded; loves trading info and odds, casually well-informed about other teams
- Standing: +1 for trading banter/info in kind; he'll also drop a free useful tidbit about a rival team regardless of roll
- Knows that they will be the first match starting tomorrow, and expects a challenge.

**Castellan Hadrin Voss**
- Retired Trials champion, now judge; aloof, openly skeptical of "this year's crop"
- Hates insincere flattery, respects genuine skill or wit
- Standing: +1 if the party matches his intensity and tells him to pay attention to them, -1 if they are polite and back down at his condescension

**Xavi (Bellhop)**
- Young, new hire, ambient presence throughout the manse
