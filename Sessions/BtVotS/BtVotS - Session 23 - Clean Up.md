## Post Chuck Wall Town
In the aftermath of the fight with Big Chuck, the party are in the middle of the Hexagon cage surrounded by confused fans. They all believed Big Chuck would win, but what they believe to be the impossible has been done.
### Proclamation of Victory
Give the party a moment to speak to the crowd. Most seem to watch in awe, as they are waiting for a proclamation of victory, and more importantly, what will happen to them.

### The Fang Swoop In
After a few moments, there is commotion in the crowds.
- Several Fang Enforcers are entering through the restaurant connected to the Viper's Nest. Leading them is Merra Suhlee

**Merra Suhlee** : (Head Security)
*50s. Sandy blonde and braided hair. Half-armor. Scar across lip. Blunt. Hates magic from deep arcane burn scar under her clothes*
- Here to take control of Wall Town under the orders of her Lady Usah, not only for the interest of The Fang but because of the deal with Rennin III Meredane as well.
- Directs a few enforcers to spread out and look for distinct members. They find many higher members, including two captains: [Fortuna Mortem] and [Stonecrusher Orson]
- Will work in tandem with the Thundercats, offering to take full command and control of the situation. "You've already done the hard part. This is a regular day for us. Go on get your rest. We can patch you up at the Viper's Nest or you can go home."

**Fortuna Mortem**
*A skinny human with gaudy clothes and a permanent smile. He runs the gambling and social aspects of the Gravel Rats.*
- Taken a fall, has a major road rash across his hand and face
- Begs to be let go through increasingly desperate measures
	- Apologizes to Lucky and the party
	- Admits he faked Lucky's horse race
	- Tries to bribe with half of his personal stash of gold and jewels (quickly pivots to all)

**Stonecrusher Orson**
*A dwarf with heavy burn scars, a shattered nose, and a gruff voice*
- He was found working in the forges, a bit away from Wall Town near the Underdocks
- He is very silent and does not seem to fight back, knowing that Big Chuck lost and he cannot escape
- Stonecrusher was honor-bound to serve Big Chuck, but not anymore. He will accept any punishment thrown his way.

### Explore Manse de Mortem
Rennin I Meredane uses a sending stone around this point to contact the party. 
- Congratulates the party on victory
- On the topic of the automatons, the party can secure them and leave them with The Fang if need be. But, he won't say no to another set of eyes looking around for them. 
- Payment will be delivered the moment they have been secured.
Find the Meredane Automatons
- If the party elects to leave early, The Fang WILL find the automatons in the Wall Town Manor. The party will receive 5000 gold pieces and 500 platinum.
- If the party elects to search themselves, they will either destroy or turn them in.
	- Upon destruction, Merra Suhlee will enter the room and see what has happened. Grateful for dealing with Chuck, she will not strike the party down, but reprimand them for a stupid decision and consistently making enemies. The party receives NO reward and makes an enemy of House Meredane.

The automatons are located within the [[Manse de Mortem]]
