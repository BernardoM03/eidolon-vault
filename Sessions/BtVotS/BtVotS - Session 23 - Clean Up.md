## Post Chuck Wall Town
In the aftermath of the fight with Big Chuck, the party are in the middle of the Hexagon cage surrounded by confused fans. They all believed Big Chuck would win, but what they believe to be the impossible has been done.
### Proclamation of Victory
Give the party a moment to speak to the crowd. Most seem to watch in awe, as they are waiting for a proclamation of victory, and more importantly, what will happen to them.

### The Fang Swoop In
After a few moments, there is commotion in the crowds.
- Several Fang Enforcers are entering through the restaurant connected to the Viper's Nest. Leading them is Merra Suhlee

**Merra Suhlee** : (Head Security)
*50s. Sandy blonde and braided hair. Half-armor. Scar across lip. Blunt. Hates magic from deep arcane burn scar under her clothes*
- Here to take control of Wall Town under the orders of her Lady Usah, not only for the interest of The Fang but because of the deal with Rennin III Meredane as well.
- Directs a few enforcers to spread out and look for distinct members. They find many higher members, including two captains: [Fortuna Mortem] and [Stonecrusher Orson]
- Will work in tandem with the Thundercats, offering to take full command and control of the situation. "You've already done the hard part. This is a regular day for us. Go on get your rest. We can patch you up at the Viper's Nest or you can go home."

**Fortuna Mortem**
*A skinny human with gaudy clothes and a permanent smile. He runs the gambling and social aspects of the Gravel Rats.*
- Taken a fall, has a major road rash across his hand and face
- Begs to be let go through increasingly desperate measures
	- Apologizes to Lucky and the party
	- Admits he faked Lucky's horse race
	- Tries to bribe with half of his personal stash of gold and jewels (quickly pivots to all)

**Stonecrusher Orson**
*A dwarf with heavy burn scars, a shattered nose, and a gruff voice*
- He was found working in the forges, a bit away from Wall Town near the Underdocks
- He is very silent and does not seem to fight back, knowing that Big Chuck lost and he cannot escape
- Stonecrusher was honor-bound to serve Big Chuck, but not anymore. He will accept any punishment thrown his way.

## Short Rest Before the Night
This will be a moment where the party has the time to rest before going to the Castella Grande tonight. 
Getting paid by the Meredane family
### Brennly Wriggle
*An old halfling man, reddened nose, messy combover with full head of white hair, chin curtain beard, preppy outfit with a vest and a large tool belt*

Brennly, now after his rest and travel back, has made it home to Catameria. He strolled home bag over one shoulder and a wrapped pastry in hand, only to find his estate has been reduced to rubble. He is devastated, not crying but lost in thought. 

When he finally sees the party, he scans for Atar, Cogsworth, and the others he met in the village. "Oh good. You're all still in one piece." His sadness is immediately replaced by relief. And he also greets the others.

He would apologize first:
-  "I should have told you what I knew before any of this started. I thought I was being careful. I thought I had it contained." A pause. "I am very sorry. For all of it."

Information that Brennly has:
- He knew that something regarding devils was occurring in Sage's Hollow, as it was something told to him by the Pride Epitome. He went there to investigate, but felt as though he had to keep it secret.
- Knows little about the Grey King artifact and how to use it. As an artificer, and with many strongly invested magical items, he would recommend plenty of study and attunement to align yourself closer with the artifact. Scholars would know that legendary and powerful artifacts will always have their own ego and personality, so you have to grapple with that. 

## Castella Grande
*A grand manse commonly used by the [[Merion]] Royal family for hosting events and important guests for multi-day events.* 

### Approaching + Exterior
The party travels through the Verdure District headed in the direction of the Stone Crown. It is the late afternoon, so the sun has already set. A frost is in the air, and the first snowflakes begin to fall.
- Castella Grande looks like an extravagant villa at the end of a long road lined by olive trees stripped bare by the season.
- The building itself is creamy pale stone, two full stories plus a tiled roof of deep terracotta. Despite the wintery season, it retains a sense of warmth
- When breathing, you can see the mist of your breath
- Royal Guards are posted across the entire venue, with seemingly tightened security as well

**Guard House**
Before the villa, there is a surrounding fence and small black-slate guard house. Inside, an old goliath with glasses with a newspaper.  He is like Mike Ehrmentraut
- In this world, goliaths are native to the mountains south of Catameria, a similar area to the ancient society where Cogsworth is from. They are said to be blessed by Korrad, a mountain spirit, who transformed the original people to become bigger, stronger, and sturdier to survive the harsh conditions.
### Main Foyer
*The foyer is where the party is formally received. A senior steward, Terrence, greets them, already informed of their arrival. The check-in is brief and efficient.*
- The floor is polished burnt orange and terracotta tiles, warmed by in-laid heating enchantments beneath the stones
-  The room is lit with iron wall sconces set at chest height that run the length of both side walls, enough to brightly illuminate the room with a soft amber.
- There are a variety of plants from virtually any corner of Ennalis
- A wide central staircase rises at the far end, twin flights curving up and inward to meet at a landing beneath a tall arched window
- When the sun is right, the courtyard garden turns the whole foyer golden**

**Terrence, Senior Steward of Castella Grande**
*Charming and forward steward/concierge of the Castella Grande. He is not from wealth, but has grew to claim and enjoy it. He will always side with what he believes is right*
- Roleplay him like M. Gustave from Budapest hotel
- He would be happy to give a tour in the morning, but he must prepare from the Ball tonight.
- He will send his young bellhop to guide the party to their rooms
### Hallways
*Nico, a young bellhop hired just this week, escorts the party to their Guest Room Suite through this hallway*
- The walkway continues with the orange terracotta tiles with warmth-enchanted tiles
- The corridor is about 5 feet wide and the ceilings are a bit lower down, with exposed dark wood beams supporting the architecture at intervals
- The walls are are plastered with a warm finish. Hung at intervals with dark-framed portraits and oil paintings: landscapes mostly, a few formal portraits in older dress.
- Walking past every turn, you see at least one staff member cleaning or moving. 
- With a high perception, as you pass by guest rooms, you can tell that the walls are thick enough that you can't overhear conversations behind closed doors. You reason that this is by design.

- The door opens to a common area with a stone fireplace, a table, and chairs to situate the party.
- There are a set of keys for each of you, that open the door to the suite
- This common area separates into three semi open bed rooms, no doors or solid wall between here and the common area, but  can be blocked by a set of curtains. Two beds are in each room. 
- On each bed, you see a wrapped package embroidered with the manor's iconography. A set of loaned clothing
	- Inside is a set of Shift-weave that will allow the party to decorate and disguise how they would like for the Ball later.
