### Outcomes
**Evil Route**: This is the easy option. The work has been done, and the reward is ready to be reaped by all included parties. House Meredane will continue to work in the shadows for a few years, with Rennin I fully resurrected. Over about a decade, He will take control over the house with Rennin III as a stand in, but eventually toss him aside to rule himself. A new age of the world will be brought through Rennin I's industrialization, propelling Catameria into a global economic and military superpower. The Usah family have bought their way into the Elite with this decision, which will eventually make them one of the richest families in the world. The party will generally be forgotten, but could make for useful employees in the future.

**Unfortunate Route**: The party upsets both the Fang and the Gravel Rats. That will not go unpunished. They will seek to embarrass the party, planting poisons and assassins. Spreading rumors to guild owners, nobility, and people in power. The party will have a hard time working under these conditions, being tired and without help. Perhaps the only ally on their side will be Baato. Eventually, the party will be driven out of the city

**Fang Route**: The party will be able to make a deal with the Fang. The party will relinquish control over their Royal Boons to The Fang. In return, they will be sponsored and given a stronger opportunity to win the Winter Trials. The Fang, in their power with Lady Usah's charisma, will be able to convince the Meredane family that the automatons were rigged with explosives and no longer obtainable. 

**Scot-Free Route**: The party is able to get out scot-free. They don't get any payment from House Meredane, but they are able to stop Rennin from attaining the automatons at the current moment.
