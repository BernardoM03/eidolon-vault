## The Golem Problem
Given the situation, the party will have to grapple with the decisions before them. They can do the easy thing, profit for working alongside the Meredane family and maintain connections with two powerful noble families, or they can do the hard thing and trust their gut and sabotage the deal.

### The Golems
Located in a small set of boxes within Wall Town. They are not being closely watched or guarded at the moment, but there are Fang guards nearby. Most particularly, Merra Suhlee is nearby, who will be an incredible threat to the party at the moment.

ANY and I mean ANY harm against the automatons out of nowhere will meet the party with suspicion, especially considering their most recent request to Captain Suhlee. On the other hand, framing the act or perhaps getting a Gravel Rat to sabotage it would be believable. A blast big enough to full render the automatons unusable will need to be a sizeable explosion, possibly around force or bludgeoning 40 damage

### The Viper's Nest
*Black-varnished tables, low lights, imported glassware. Circular bar at center. Silver Fang insignia everywhere. This bar is a sign of wealth and ambience.*

**Lady Sesharra Usah** : (Boss)
*Early 60s. Straight black hair. Dark-green dress. Calm, unhurried, unreadable.*
- Congratulates the party on a job well-done. 
- Intrigued to hear more about a proposition to stop the deal with the Meredane by taking the robots for themselves.
- Tries to not let on much about the deal between the Usah and Meredane families, but says that it will allow them control and a foothold into control of the future.
- Lady Usah will propose that she will agree to such a deal, but given the fact that the party will relinquish control over their Royal Boons to The Fang. In return, they will be sponsored and given a stronger opportunity to win as well.
### Outcomes
**Evil Route**: This is the easy option. The work has been done, and the reward is ready to be reaped by all included parties. House Meredane will continue to work in the shadows for a few years, with Rennin I fully resurrected. Over about a decade, He will take control over the house with Rennin III as a stand in, but eventually toss him aside to rule himself. A new age of the world will be brought through Rennin I's industrialization, propelling Catameria into a global economic and military superpower. The Usah family have bought their way into the Elite with this decision, which will eventually make them one of the richest families in the world. The party will generally be forgotten, but could make for useful employees in the future.

**Unfortunate Route**: The party upsets both the Fang and the Gravel Rats. That will not go unpunished. They will seek to embarrass the party, planting poisons and assassins. Spreading rumors to guild owners, nobility, and people in power. The party will have a hard time working under these conditions, being tired and without help. Perhaps the only ally on their side will be Baato. Eventually, the party will be driven out of the city

**Fang Route**: The party will be able to make a deal with the Fang. The party will relinquish control over their Royal Boons to The Fang. In return, they will be sponsored and given a stronger opportunity to win the Winter Trials. The Fang, in their power with Lady Usah's charisma, will be able to convince the Meredane family that the automatons were rigged with explosives and no longer obtainable. 

**Scot-Free Route**: The party is able to get out scot-free. They don't get any payment from House Meredane, but they are able to stop Rennin from attaining the automatons at the current moment.
