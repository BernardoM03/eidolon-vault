### Fight in the Dark
I want to create a difficult fight for 5 x lvl5 PCs. This will take place in a stadium but enshrouded in magical darkness. This magical darkness can be combatted with leveled light-emitting spells, such as daylight or faerie fire. 

Generally, this is powerful. The darkness is avoided and the sunlight sensitivity is activated for the drider. Because of this, I want to add either a new trait or a few new enemies to this encounter...

Light Eating Fairies. These are tiny creatures that, after a certain period of time, eat light. This creates a 2-phase battle: 

Phase 1: An amped up drider can whittle the party down with its strong attacks and spells, despite there being light/sunlight. The sources of get eaten by the Light-Eating Fairies, causing party members to continuously use spell slots to generate light to avoid magical darkness. Once the fairies have eaten enough light, they become a powerful enemy as well. 

Phase 2: The fairies have consumed enough light and coalesce to form a large powerful hivemind. The darkness is not much of an issue anymore, considering the fairies emit it themselves. Any casts of light emitting spells like faerie fire only serve to benefit the fairies. In addition, radiant spells like Guiding Bolt do not give advantage, and slightly benefit the fairies.
### Drider Tactics
Start by using movement and Shadow Step to disappear from the arena. This drider is intelligent enough that she will use the darkness and stealth to have the party lose track of her. Roll advantage with stealth, and reemerge and the beginning of her turn to attack a vulnerable party member, effectively beginning the fight.

### Apothite Faeries
3x fairies 
30hp
don't attack but will eat light sources
Guiding Bolt 1x charge
Daylight 3x charge
Light or Dancing Lights 1x Charge
Faerie Fire 1x Charge

Once the fairies have consumed 3 charges, they can no longer consume any charges. They must expel the light in a burst. All creatures within 20ft of the fairies must make a DC 14 Constitution save or take 4d6 Radiant damage and be blinded. Half damage on success.
### Attack on the Stadium
Outside of the first combat onto the party here, there will be another attack on the stands, separate from the Winter Trials entirely.

On the first round of combat at Initiative 0, a large explosion will be heard about 60 ft away from Twila. This will be followed by screams. 

This attack is a joint coordinated attack between House Meredane and the Gravel Rats, with possible smuggling ties to Renoux. Most of this evidence will be well covered up though. Left of the scene, will be a piece of scrap metal that hinges as an automaton. 

- What Happened?: A automaton was disguised as a regular civilian, and used as a bomb to plant into the stands. The bomb was specifically designed by a powder smith in the undercity, used to fatally injure and display a message.
