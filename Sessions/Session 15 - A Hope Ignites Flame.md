### Brief session summary
The Thundercats easily completed the first round of [[The Winter Trials]], making their way onto the second round on the second day. Before heading home, a bit of social interaction and partying was in order, as you all head to the Laughing Gauntlet's waiting room to hang out for a bit.

Heading back to [[Brennly's Home]], Atar wanted to find out a bit more about why Brennly was warned to him. After investigating his study, you found powerful and ancient magical artifacts. A devil's grimoire he notes as the "Pride Epitome," a sentient tome. The petrified phalange of the Divine Emperor of the Ruined Greylinn Empire, the Grey King. And a pair of soul coins wrapped inside a letter written in infernal. You don't know what to make of this just yet, but you do know that Brennly was tied to a devil's plot and likely needs help.

Jasper, you aren't too comfortable with these people yet, so you decided to sleep in the Colosseum of Heroes for the night. In the walk back, you encountered a tired, drunk, agitated wreck of a man. While you did not catch his name or recognize him, he belonged to the House Meredane. This scuffle between the two seemed to have summoned a freakishly large brute from invisibility, ending the encounter with each of you going your separate ways.

Finally, the morning of the second day arrived. Adelaide, you took your time to make a nice breakfast for everyone. Lucky, you built a bond between you and your father, inviting him to watch today's game. And now it is time to start the second battle. The party before you battled a Giant Squid while the stadium was filled with water. As the water drained, you made your way to the stadium floor, approaching the commentator's stand above you. Iyra Valcaryn, a mage of House Valcaryn, the most powerful mage family in Catameria had designed this next encounter to test you. But remember this is only the 2nd stage, as the hardest has yet to come.

8 pillars of black slate stone erupted from the floor around you in the stadium.. The stadium floor's began to swirl in a vortex of wind, getting faster and hotter by the second. Released by the hand of the 7th stage mage herself, a flaming elemental screamed into the sky sparking into an explosion of flames. It shot back down and entered the vortex, increasing the speed heat of the wind to an unbearable degree. You won't last long unless you can stop this heat and take down that elemental.

The second battle is now underway. An unwavering flame erupts from a small hole in each of the pillars, one by one.

## The Heat of Battle
view Falindrith's for the Murder Comet (Blazing State)'s statblock.

The pillars can be deactivated by putting out the flame in each of the pillars. Once all 8 pillars are deactivated, the Murder Comet will be stunned and crash into the ground, making it easy to kill. 

Before the final blow, the Murder Comet will look towards the closest party member, and speak in perfect common with a feminine voice. "Thundercats, I've got something to tell you. This creature is stunned for the time being, so do not worry you have won. To be safe, have one or two of your party celebrate victory, as the rest of you hear me out." If the party is hesitant as first, INSIST immediately in a serious tone.

The person speaking through this is Iyra Valcaryn, and she wants to talk to the party about Meredane. She tells the party to meet her at a bar known as the "Grey Lance" in upper Catavale North at sundown tonight.

### The Grey Lance
This tavern/bar/restaurant is a themed environment. It is a cultural recreation the society at the later end of the Divine Greylinn Empire. 

The walk to this tavern isn't far from "Brennly's Home/Colosseum/Temple." The outside streets are comprised of groups of people walking, either going to their favorite restaurant or store or going from work to home. It's a bit of a lively environment. You arrive to this large odd-looking building, the architecture completely separate from the rest of the street. White/grey sandstone walls with ornate chiseling, but based on marks and patterns, it definitely seems to be magically shaped. 

Inside, you can see dozens of people sitting around the tavern room. The flame from the hearth dimly lights the stoney design of the room, offsetting the bright gray with the comforting low lights and warmth. One table large enough to fit all of you seems to be open and empty, with what seems to be a shepherd dog lying down beside. (The dog is Iyra Valcaryn)

Irya wishes to speak about Cogsworth, and wants the party to find more about the Meredane. Go to Halandar's Antiques, a store between the Merial Docks and the Promenade.
