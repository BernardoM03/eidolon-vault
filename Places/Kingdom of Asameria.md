The Kingdom of Asameria is a nation in the center of western Ennalis, ruled by the longstanding line of the [[Merion]] monarchy. Asameria was founded as a small settlement at Altor Merion.

## A Kingdom at War
The Kingdom of Asameria exists in a long-standing feud with other states in western Ennalis. More of this can be found in [[Warring States]].
