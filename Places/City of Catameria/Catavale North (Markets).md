Catavale North is the heart of [[Catameria]] for it's residents. Streets wind between open-air markets, theaters, specialty shops, restaurants, and artisans. This ward’s bustling streets and compact buildings make it a prime location for middle-low income housing for the honest workers of the city. In addition, being the center of the city makes it a prime location for buildings of spectacle, such as the [[Grand Arena]], the [[Rennin Bank]], and [[La Galerie]]. 
Use: shopping, entertainment, tourism, work, food, crafts, low-middle class residential
Population: ~15,000
