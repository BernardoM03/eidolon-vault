Vurn Orleth, the Grand Seneschal of Catameria. Steward to the House Merion. Wielder of the King's Oath.

Vurn is a large, older man, likely in his late 60s but carrying himself like someone a decade younger. Broad shoulders, close-cropped grey hair, and a calm that reads more as control than ease. His left eye is clouded and pale, the magical eye that reads truth. It does not move the same way his right does.

In his younger years he ran an Adventurers Union, the kind of organization that handled contracts, grievances, and the protection of smaller parties getting squeezed out by wealthier patrons. He spent decades in that world before the Merion family brought him in, specifically because he was the only person they trusted to not be for sale.

He has held the office long enough to watch four noble families attempt plots against the Crown. He stopped all of them quietly. 

His relationship with Queen Althaea is one of deep loyalty, and her illness is visibly weighing on him. He will not discuss it unless pressed, and even then he will be brief.
