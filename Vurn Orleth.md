Vurn Orleth, the Grand Seneschal of Catameria. Steward to the House Merion. Wielder of the King's Oath.

Vurn Orleth is a tall, gruff, and well-put together man. He is kind, understanding, and incredibly wise. In his younger years he was a Union Leader of Adventurers, and has fought many perils. 

Vurn Orleth can always tell a truth from a lie, that much is permitted to him by his magical eye, bound to him from his adventures as a blessing and a curse. This has streamlined Catamerian politics, and singlehandedly stopped many plots of Corruption against the Merion Monarchy.
