## The Divine Tower of Greylinn
This tower, alongside Palecrown, were where the [[Grey King]] existed during the time of his reign. Very little is known about the Divine Tower, despite the fact that is exists in the heart of the [[Boz'Kumlar Desert]]. The Divine Tower was a place of reverence in the olden days, where many traveled to exercise their prayers and worship to their God King. In the fall of the Grey King and the empire, the Tower grew protective. Those who would travel to worship at it's steps found themselves unable to approach, no matter how far they walked. All adventurers who attempt the journey now either give up when they cannot approach further, or are never heard from again.

### Treasures of the Tower
- Some believe the Tower holds the mechanism of that immortality
- Artifacts of the Grey King will hold great power and knowledge
- Pilgrims and Devout Worshippers would offer gold, gems, relics, and sacred objects as tribute. Those vaults will still contain the centuries of accumulated wealth.
