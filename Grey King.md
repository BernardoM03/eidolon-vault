The Grey King was the holy emperor and Godking of the [[Divine Empire of Greylinn]]. Not much is known about the Grey King, as his presence was heavily obscured inside the [[Divine Tower of Greylinn]]. 

## Personality
The Grey King is not like many mortals. Although he used to be a regular human, he ascended to his position very early in his life and very quickly, meaning he did not have the opportunity to grow as a mortal. He grew up as a god, a superior being.

The Grey King is kind, but he is not polite. He is a god and he is a king, and he WILL be treated as such. Disrespect is not tolerated, but reverence will be rewarded with his protection. In his time as a Godking, he helped many, but he could not help them all. 
- Now that the Grey King does not have an empire to uphold, his attention can be directed more individually. This goes for each version of him embedded in the different pieces of the [[Grey King's Body]] artifacts.
