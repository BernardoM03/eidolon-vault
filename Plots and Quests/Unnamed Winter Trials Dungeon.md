This will be a 5-room dungeon run as one of the trials for the Winter Trials. The tone will be almost paying homage and parodying the basic fantasy dungeon.

### The Setting
A grand illusion veils across the arena, transforming your environment entirely. You find yourselves within the deep neck of a mildly swampy coniferous forest, along a lightly-walked trail. The trees tower over you, but the blue sky and light from the sun peaks through. Before you, a large tower stands a few hundred feet away. 
### Room 1: The Guardian Puzzle
A Riverlands Moss-Troll seems to be patrolling the front entrance. Across his belt, a large key.

**Tower Entrance**
- Massive and intricate stone door. 
	- In the center, there is a massive key hole. The key held by the Troll does not work.
