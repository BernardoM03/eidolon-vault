### Brief
The party has been transported back in time to the [[Divine Empire of Greylinn]], the Pinnacle of [[Ennalis]]. It is a bastion of strength under the sacred law granted upon the land by his Greatness, the illustrious and eternal, the Grey King.

### Player Information
This is in the same world, but over 1000 years in the past during the reign of the Grey King, known to be a near-immortal and deific god emperor.
