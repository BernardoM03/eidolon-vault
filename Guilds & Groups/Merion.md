The Merion Monarchy is a long and prestigious line of nobility ruling over the [[Kingdom of Asameria]], with a branch family descending from Cata Merion ruling over [[Catameria]]. 

The Merion's came into power through the adventures of Altor Merion. Altor was from a small village from the plains, ruled over by the [[Divine Empire of Greylinn]]. Altor's village was poor. The lands were dry, and the few crops they did manage to produce were heavily taxed by the Greylinn empire.
