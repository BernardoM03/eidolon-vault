_Oswyn,_

The day I arrived in this city, you told me that every contract contains a weakness if one is patient enough to look for it. I find myself reflecting on that lesson more often than I care to admit.

My situation with Amon remains unresolved. The debt he proclaims grows tiresome, though the terms themselves are clear enough. One hundred and eight souls. 

Your outward knowledge of infernal law and history may be something I have need of. Should you encounter any knowledge amongst your study discussing loopholes or precedents involving the reassignment of bound souls, I would value your insight.

You have always understood the importance of both of your worlds. I suspect that is why we have remained on such agreeable terms.

May fate bring us together again.

_Baeldric_
