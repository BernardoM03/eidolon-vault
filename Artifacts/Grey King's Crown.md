One of the many vestiges left behind of the Grey King of the [[Divine Empire of Greylinn]]. This crown is said to be a part of the Grey King himself, never separated until his death and the fall of the empire. 

### Stats
Dormant. +1 to all saving throws.
Awakened. +2 to all saving throws. Your lifespan is extended immensely, for every 10 years you only age 1.
Exalted. +3 to all saving throws. You no longer age.
