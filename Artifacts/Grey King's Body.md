After the fall of the [[Divine Empire of Greylinn]], calcified appendages seemed to be uncovered across different ruins of Ennalis. After significant study, these were found to be pieces of the [[Grey King]] himself, holding fragments of his godly power. 

### Grey King's Finger
Currently Held By: [[Atar]]
This is a tiny fragment of the Grey King's body. Even still, as a portion of a living deity's corpse, it holds a considerable amount of power. When attuned, this artifact offers the user access to a daily casting of the spell [Geas]([Geas - D&D 5e (2024)](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:geas)).
