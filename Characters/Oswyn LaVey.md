Oswyn LaVey is an old tiefling diabolist. He was born a tiefling to a rich lordship from the countryside in the [[Roanese Dominion]]. There was much discrimination torwards him as a child, but he was raised with a stable household and a love for knowledge. Oswyn was always curious about his nature, so he followed in the footsteps of diabolist and extraplanar scholars amongst his student days, gaining scholarly and academic praise amongst his peers. He was a scholar at the [[Königskreuz University]] for most of his early adulthood, and became a professor at a young age. He was eventually exiled for political reasons, and found footing within Catameria. He has run a business ever since.

He knows a lot about the city, and those in it, including the nature of [[Baato]], who is commonly known as Baeldric Vauldra. He will charge the party an exorbitant amount of gold, 5000, to dispel the Devil's Mark from Lucky. Atar or Twila will KNOW that this is an extreme amount.

"Do not play the fool with me. I have known devils my entire life, and I have damn near been treated like one every where I've stepped foot. Dispelling that damned curse is easy, dealing with whatever follows is what that is paying for."
