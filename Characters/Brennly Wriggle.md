### Appearance
An old halfling man (roughly 130 years old), reddened nose, messy combover with full head of white hair, chin curtain beard, preppy outfit with a vest and a large tool belt
### Roleplaying
Excited about any little talking point that others brings up, not very good at bringing up his own topics, reassures the party that they should follow their gut, grateful that they still care enough to look after him in his old age.
### Background
Wealthy background who fell out of his family ways due to a moral conflict, found his own people who fulfill his lifestyle, lived a long and moderately difficult life but he found peace living within [[Catameria]]. See [[Brennly's Home]] to see about where he lives.

More recently, he has come into contact with the magical tome Pride Epitome, a sentient host for the Archdevil [[Amon]]. Brennly's research into the artifact brought him dangerously close to the realm of devils, and into the plans of Amon himself, which inadvertently started the events of the campaign by bringing the party to the doorstep of [[Baato]]'s plan in [[Sage's Hollow]].
