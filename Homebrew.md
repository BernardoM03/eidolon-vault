## Spells
### Geas in Public
Geas requires a verbal command with a casting time of 1 minute to effectively charm a targeted creature. This casting time is done by sprinkling key words that hold arcane power into conversation, that slowly permeate the target's mind until the final phrase is uttered, causing the spell to take effect. To use this spell stealthily in public, the target must roll a Deception (CHA) against a target's Arcana (INT).
- Caster Wins: Target has no idea a spell was cast
- Target Wins: Target realizes a spell is being cast with certain key words

The Caster may have advantage if:
- They've been talking to the target for 10+ minutes before casting
- The target is relaxed, drunk, or distracted
- The Geas aligns with something the target already wants to do
The Target may have advantage if:
- They're suspicious of the caster already
- A friendly creature is present to here the spell
