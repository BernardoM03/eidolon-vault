### Brief
The Boz'Kumlar Desert extends across the North-Western shores of [[Ennalis]], currently occupying the spaces of the [[Empire of Balat]] and the [[Divine Empire of Guilland]], and between the borders. The environment of the Desert aren't survivable conditions by most mortals, making it unfit to sustain and occupy by any empire. The only empire known to have had a consistent hold on the land was the now-fallen [[Divine Empire of Greylinn]]. 

The Boz'Kumlar Desert contains countless unique flora and fauna. A notable few creatures are the Sand Giants, Dune Driders, Sand Worms, and Sand Elementals. A notable few plants are the Black Poppy, the Kumlari Cactus, and various succulents.
